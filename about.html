Mission Statement

The mission of the Vietnamese Student Association is to promote Vietnamese culture and heritage at Notre Dame. VSA hopes to accomplish this by having events throughout the year which may include Vietnamese food or lectures/research talks. In addition, VSA organizes the Anh Chi Em program, a family program where VSA club members are placed into groups to foster bonding and community.

Goals for 2016

    - Host the first-ever VSA culture show in the Fall 2016 semester
    - Begin preparation to attend the VIA-1 conference next year and the future
